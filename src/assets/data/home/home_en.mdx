# Welcome to the ERAS Research Group!

*Efficient, Reliable, and Advanced System Research Group*

Our research group, led by Prof. Zhirong Shen, primarily focuses on intelligent storage technologies. Specifically, our research interests includes reliable and efficient storage based on erasure codes, fault tolerance and performance optimization for emerging memory devices, KV storage systems as well as LLM system.

In the past four years, our group has published over ten papers in international conferences and authoritative journals, including PPoPP, USENIX ATC, HPCA, SC, INFOCOM, ICDE and IEEE TC, ACM TOS, ACM TACO. Additionally, we have received support from national grants, such as the Natural Science Foundation of China, and have established collaborations with renowned companies like Alibaba and Huawei.
